American Lasagna

Description

American Lasagna is a classic baked dish known for its rich layers and bold, comforting flavors.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef (or Italian sausage)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (24-ounce) jar tomato sauce or marinara sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning (or a mix of dried basil, oregano, thyme)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg (optional, to mix with ricotta)
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon sugar (optional, to cut acidity)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the noodles: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  3. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions until al dente.
  4. Drain and lay flat on a baking sheet or drizzle lightly with oil to prevent sticking.
  5. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  6. Add chopped onion and sauté until translucent (about 3-4 minutes).
  7. Add minced garlic and cook for another 30 seconds.
  8. Add ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon. Drain excess fat if needed.
  9. Stir in tomato sauce, Italian seasoning, sugar (if using), salt, and pepper.
  10. Let the sauce simmer on low for about 10 minutes.
  11. In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese with the egg (if using) and a pinch of salt. Mix well.
  12. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  13. Layer 4 noodles over the sauce.
  14. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
  15. Sprinkle 1 cup mozzarella over the ricotta.
  16. Add about 1/3 of the meat sauce over the cheese layer.
  17. Repeat the layers two more times (noodles, ricotta, mozzarella, meat sauce).
  18. Finish with a final layer of noodles topped with remaining meat sauce and sprinkle Parmesan cheese on top.
  19. Cover with aluminum foil (to prevent cheese from burning).
  20. Bake for 25 minutes, then remove the foil and bake an additional 20-25 minutes until bubbly and golden on top.
  21. Let the lasagna sit for about 10-15 minutes before cutting. This helps it set and makes serving easier.
